Milestones of Space

First Moon Landing

On July 20, 1969, Apollo 11 astronauts Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin became the first humans to set foot on the moon.

Launch of Sputnik 1

Launch of Sputnik 1 in 1957 marked the dawn of the space age, propelling humanity into the era of satellite communication and exploration

Apollo 11 Moon Landing

The Apollo 11 moon landing on July 20, 1969, marked the first time humans set foot on the moon, with astronauts Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin making history.

Voyager 1 and 2

The Voyager 1 and 2 spacecrafts, launched in 1977, embarked on a groundbreaking mission to explore the outer planets of our solar system and continue their journey into interstellar space, providing invaluable data and images of distant worlds.

First image of black hole

The first image of a black hole, captured by the Event Horizon Telescope in 2019, provided unprecedented visual confirmation of Einstein's theory of general relativity and offered insights into the extreme gravitational forces at the heart of these enigmatic cosmic objects.
