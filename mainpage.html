About

Our project, ASTROSPACE, is a pioneering web application designed to bring the wonders of astronomy closer to users. By leveraging real-time data and user location, ASTROSPACE delivers timely notifications for celestial events observable from the user's surroundings, fostering a deeper connection with the cosmos. With a focus on accessibility and education, our platform aims to inspire curiosity and appreciation for the wonders of the universe.
